INTRODUCTION

Chakras are the seven points within one’s self where your soul can travel and in perfect alignment can grant an individual divine power. The three in the lower body deal with one’s primitive nature. While, the four in the upper body engage with one’s spiritual nature. When aligned, one’s primitive and spiritual nature conjoin and peace with self is obtained.

Moreover, the 1st point is the root chakra which is identified by the color red, the 2nd point is the sacral chakra which is identified by the color orange, the 3rd point is the solar plex chakra which is identified by the color yellow, the 4th point is the heart chakra which is identified by the color green, the 5th point is the throat chakra which is identified by the color blue, the 6th point is the third eye chakra which is identified by the color indigo, and the 7th point is the crown chakra which is identified by the color purple. Further, the Chakra concept represents who we were, are, and can be. It is a metaphysical explanation on the general inner workings of the Huemind. As the different levels of the chakras offer explanations and solutions to individuals operating at the lowest or highest frequencies.
