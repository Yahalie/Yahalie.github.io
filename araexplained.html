T H Y | C O N C E P T

The ARA concept stands for Attainment, Retainment, and Application. Attainment is the process of achieving educational growth. Retainment is the process of keeping the newly found education in one’s mind, or keeping yourself engaged. While, Application is the action of putting the new-learned knowledge into operation. So, Attainment + Retainment + Application = the proper education of the uneducated. In other words, the ARA concept provides you with a path to learning some of the most complex concepts. You will first be taught the information at this stage you simply intake the information, this stage is simple. The next stage you will be forced to think outside the box in order to help remember the information. Last, you will inevitably operate at a higher frequency as you make an attempt to apply the new-found information. So, at attainment there will be an article; Retainment a visual; and application, instructions as you go down the ARA path.
